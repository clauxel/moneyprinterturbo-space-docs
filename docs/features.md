# Features - MoneyPrinterTurbo Studio

MoneyPrinterTurbo Studio scores topic, script, search terms, voice, subtitles, aspect ratio, and review state before a short-video job moves into production.

## Topic readiness scoring

Check hook clarity, audience fit, length, CTA, and risk before a creator spends rendering time.

## Script and scene review

Turn a draft prompt or script into scene count, length estimate, and missing dependency notes.

## Stock-source planning

Map search terms and source policy so a job does not fail later because media assumptions were vague.

## Voice and subtitle alignment

Review language, TTS expectation, subtitle placement, and format before export.

## Batch queue planning

Prepare multiple short-video jobs with consistent settings and review receipts.

## Paid export receipts

Use checkout to unlock saved history, team approval, and shareable job evidence.

## Pricing and Support

Plans cover short-video runs, batch planning, team review, subtitle and TTS checks, hosted previews, and export receipts.
